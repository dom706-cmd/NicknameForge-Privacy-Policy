Privacy Policy for NicknameForge

Effective Date: January 8, 2026

1. Data Collection

NicknameForge does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All data you enter (names, descriptions, nicknames) is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers.

2. Image Usage

When using Pro features, NicknameForge fetches random images from Unsplash's public API. These images are downloaded directly to your device and are not stored by us. Image usage is governed by Unsplash's terms of service and licenses.

3. Purchases

Pro features are purchased through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. All purchase data is handled by Apple.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Unsplash API to fetch images. Their privacy policy can be found at unsplash.com/privacy.

5. Children's Privacy

NicknameForge is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in app updates.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

support@nicknameforge.app
